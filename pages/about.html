About Dead Weight Fitness

Dead Weight Fitness is an apparel company that endorses charity and selects a charity each month to design a T-Shirt. All charities receive 50% of sales for their design for the month.

Founded by Jaread Parziale, Dead Weight Fitness is committed to supporting good causes while providing high-quality apparel for our customers.
