Welcome to Dead Weight Fitness

Dead Weight Fitness is an apparel company dedicated to supporting charities. Each month, we partner with a new charity to design a unique T-shirt, and 50% of the sales from these designs go directly to the charity. Our goal is to make a positive impact through fashion, by helping various organizations that are making a difference in the world.

Our Mission

Founded by Jaread Parziale, Dead Weight Fitness aims to create high-quality, stylish apparel while supporting meaningful causes. We believe that fashion can be a force for good, and we're committed to giving back to the community through our charitable partnerships.
